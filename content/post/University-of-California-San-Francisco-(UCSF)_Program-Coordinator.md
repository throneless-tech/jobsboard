The California Preterm Birth Initiative (PTBi-CA) is seeking a Program Coordinator to oversee daily operations and provide overall project coordination for several projects housed within the PTBi-CA aims. As an integral part of a professional team and under the direction of the PTBi-CA Program Directors, essential responsibilities of this position may include, but are not limited to:
•	Coordinate program planning and implementation of PTBi-CA research studies and programs and collaborate with program/project/research staff
•	Perform administrative duties such as processing payments and stipends, submitting IRBs, and processing procurements
•	Prepare, edit and modify documents including research reports, manuscripts, presentations, proposals and internal/external communications for PTBi-CA research studies and programs
•	Support various forms of meeting coordination, event coordination, project calendar management, travel coordination and reimbursements, and high level administrative support for several projects housed within the PTBi-CA aims, including but not limited to Community Engagement, Communications, etc.
•	Provide direct support for management and distribution of information that may be sensitive or confidential
•	Research and conduct analysis on complex topics and summarize data and best practices in the field.
