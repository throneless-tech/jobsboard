The Finance Manager (FM) is integral to NFPRHA's operations function. The individual will collaborate in planning and managing the accounting and finance activities of the organization, including budgeting, financial reporting, and grants management. The individual also serves as the first point of contact for NFPRHA's accounts payable and receivable functions. This individual will work in partnership with the Senior Finance Director and regularly with operations team colleagues, staff and interns across the organization, and external vendors and constituents.
