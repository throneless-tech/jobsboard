UCSF's Division of Obstetrics, Gynecology & Gynecologic Subspecialties (OGGS) is within Department of Obstetrics, Gynecology, & Reproductive Sciences. OGGS is a large division consisting of 24 faculty members, five per-diem providers, and eight clinic locations in the San Francisco Bay Area, and additional clinical presence outside the Bay Area. To learn more about the Division of Obstetrics, Gynecology & Gynecologic Subspecialties click here: https://obgyn.ucsf.edu/gynecology. OGGS is seeking a Program Coordinator. Responsibilities will include: Managing and performing administrative services and overseeing the academic operations; Managing complex time-sensitive projects on behalf of the Division Chief, Directors, and other leadership team members; Being a primary liaison to faculty, staff, and external organizations, providing executive level support in the absence of key personnel; The position is flexible and will require the employee to be onsite once day a week.
